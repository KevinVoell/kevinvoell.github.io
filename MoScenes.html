Mo Scenes

Mo Scenes gives you greater control of your WeMo® switches by allowing you to control sets of switches with just one tap.

Mo Scenes is available on the iPhone®, iPod Touch®, and iPad® and supports iPad® Slide Over and Split View, so you can control your WeMo® switches without leaving your favorite app.

Features

Scenes

  • Create customized scenes
  • Control a scene with a single tap
  • Sync scenes between devices

Switches

  • Control individual switches
Mo Scenes allows you to control individual and groups of switches with a single tap.

Create new scenes or edit existing scenes.

When creating new scenes tell Mo Scenes which switches to turn on, off, or ignore.
